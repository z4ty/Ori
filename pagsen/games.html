The Ori Series

Ori and The Blind Forest

Steam Description: “Ori and the Blind Forest” tells the story of a young orphan destined for heroism, in a visually stunning action-platformer created by Moon Studios.

  • Developer: Moon Studios
  • Publisher: Xbox Game Studios
  • Release Date: 03/11/2015
  • Mode: Single-Player
  • Genre: Adventure, Metroidvania
  • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Nintendo Switch

Ori and the Blind Forest was the first game in the Ori series. The game tells the origin of the little spirit Ori and we play through his journey to save his home after a great storm hits, causing various problems, and to discover why the Spirit Tree, which maintained the harmony of the forest, was attacked by Kuro, a giant and powerful owl.

Ori and the Blind Forest was nominated for the following awards: The Game Award for Best Art Direction - 2015; The Game Award for Best Music/Soundtrack - 2015; The Game Award for Best Action/Adventure Game - 2015; The Game Award for Best Independent Game - 2015; BAFTA Video Games Award: Artistic Achievement - 2016; BAFTA Video Games Award: Best Music - 2016; BAFTA Video Games Award: Debut Game - 2016; BAFTA Video Games Award: Original Property - 2016; AND MORE! It received the following awards: The Game Award for Best Art Direction - 2015; BAFTA Video Games Award: Artistic Achievement - 2016.

Ori and The Will of the Wisps

Steam Description: Embark on a new journey in a vast and exotic world, where you will encounter gigantic enemies and challenging puzzles in your quest to discover Ori's destiny.

  • Developer: Moon Studios
  • Publisher: Xbox Game Studios
  • Release Date: 03/11/2020
  • Mode: Single-Player
  • Genre: Adventure, Metroidvania
  • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Nintendo Switch

Ori and The Will of the Wisps is the second and most recent game in the Ori series. This time the game puts us on a journey to save his new friend and sister Ku, an owl and daughter of Kuro, to save and heal the devastated land of Niwen, and to discover Ori's true destiny.

Ori and The Will of the Wisps was nominated for the following awards: The Game Award for Best Art Direction - 2020; The Game Award for Best Music/Soundtrack - 2020; The Game Award for Best Action/Adventure Game - 2020; BAFTA Games Award: Best Music - 2021; Annie Award for Best Character Animation in a Video Game - 2021; BAFTA Games Award: Best Animation - 2021. Ori and The Will of the Wisps received the award for Best Xbox Game in 2020 at the Golden Joystick Awards.
