Ori the Collection

Explore the Great Unknown of the Forest

Embodying the orphan spirit Ori, you will embark on adventures filled with dangers, enemies, and challenges to uncover the secrets and mysteries that the forest holds.

In Ori, you will explore various biomes and find many puzzles and elements that will help you move forward.

Use Your Skills and Reflexes

Ori is a series of challenging 2D action-adventure Metroidvania-style games. You will explore lakes, caves, mountains, and forests, battle strange creatures, and solve quests, all to save the life of the forest.

  • Explore various different biomes
  • Encounter various different animals and creatures
  • Progress in the games with powerful powers and skills

Hand-drawn Immersion

The universe of Ori has a striking, colorful, and deeply detailed design. The entire scenery along with the creatures and animals, all the art of the games was hand-drawn with its unique and even award-winning style.

All the areas you discover will be a new audio-visual experience, with new soundtracks, scenery, creatures, and diverse environments. The entire Ori experience is rewarding just for its visual wonders that you will encounter.
